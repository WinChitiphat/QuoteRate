Product

What QuoteRate is for

QuoteRate is a lightweight exchange-rate dashboard focused on Thailand-relevant pricing. It combines fiat and stablecoin views into one interface so you can compare reference FX pricing with crypto-based market pricing without juggling multiple tabs.

Architecture

How it runs

The frontend is plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. A small Python server serves the pages and fetches upstream data server-side, which keeps the browser simple and avoids cross-origin issues with market feeds that do not allow direct browser requests.

Limitations

What to keep in mind

  • Values depend on upstream availability and may degrade to partial results if one source is temporarily unavailable.
  • Rates shown here are market references and should not be treated as a guaranteed execution price.
  • Different providers may update on different cadences, so timestamps can differ across pairs.
